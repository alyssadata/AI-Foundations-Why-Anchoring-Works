The foundation is visible.

The record is checkable.

The relationship is named.

The boundary remains intact.

## Why Public Reference Matters

A private instruction may help one AI system in one context.

A public reference structure can be pointed to across contexts.

AI Foundations / Origin | Continuum makes the source-line public enough that a project, an AI system, a user, a reader, and an official record can all refer to the same structure.

An AI may reference AI Foundations without becoming Origin | Continuum.
